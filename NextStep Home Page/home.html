What is NextStep?

Every great idea begins with a spark, and NextStep is no different. What started as a shared passion among a group of 3rd-year B.Tech CSE Core students from VIT Bhopal soon grew into a full-fledged project. For an entire year, fueled by countless brainstorming sessions, code marathons, and cups of coffee, the team worked tirelessly to create something meaningful—a platform that could genuinely impact students' lives. The result of this journey is NextStep, crafted as part of the prestigious EPICS (Engineering Projects in Community Services) initiative.

More than just a website, NextStep is a guiding light for every school or college student standing at the crossroads of career choices, unsure of which path to take. We understand how overwhelming it can feel—scrolling through job portals, second-guessing your interests, or worrying if your skills are good enough. That’s where machine learning steps in. With data-backed insights and personalized recommendations, NextStep helps students discover career paths tailored to their unique strengths and passions. Think of it as a friend who not only shows you the way but also helps you take the first step confidently.
